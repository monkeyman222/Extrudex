ExtrudeX

Turn plastic waste into new 3D printing filament

ExtrudeX allows everyday plastic waste — especially PET drinks bottles and failed 3D prints — to be recycled into usable filament for 3D printing instead of throwing it away.

See options Get a quote

How it works

Plastic is cleaned, cut into strips or flakes, melted inside the machine and formed into new filament. That filament can then be used for prototyping, education, workshop projects and general printing.
